PROTON MAIL DNS SETUP CHECKLIST FOR LOUDFAIR.COM
================================================

□ Step 1: Add Verification Record
  - Log into GoDaddy
  - Go to DNS management for loudfair.com
  - Add TXT record:
    Name: @
    Value: protonmail-verification=6d50e400e1ecc8b9c648c50477e351385a242bb1
  - Save

□ Step 2: Verify in Proton Mail
  - Click Verify button in Proton Mail
  - Wait 5-15 minutes if needed

□ Step 3: Add MX Records (delete any existing MX records first!)
  - MX Record 1: @ → mail.protonmail.ch (Priority: 10)
  - MX Record 2: @ → mailsec.protonmail.ch (Priority: 20)

□ Step 4: Add SPF Record
  - TXT Record: @ → v=spf1 include:_spf.protonmail.ch ~all

□ Step 5: Add DKIM Records (get values from Proton Mail)
  - CNAME: protonmail._domainkey → [Proton will provide]
  - CNAME: protonmail2._domainkey → [Proton will provide]
  - CNAME: protonmail3._domainkey → [Proton will provide]

□ Step 6: Update DMARC Record
  - Edit existing _dmarc TXT record
  - Change email to your Proton Mail address

□ Step 7: Create Email Addresses in Proton Mail
  - Go to Settings → Addresses
  - Add your desired email addresses
